The Siberian Husky

Siberians are high-energy and extremely athletic, and they can be expert escape artists.

  • Scientific name:Canis lupus familiaris
  • Average Length :51 -60cm
  • Average Lifespan :12-15years
  • Habitat :Cold climate

Huskies were developed as working sled dogs over thousands of years by the Chukchi people living in the Siberian peninsula of northeast Asia.
